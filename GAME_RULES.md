# Mafia Game - Rules & Instructions

## Overview

Mafia is a social deduction game where players are divided into two teams: **Citizens** (including special roles) and **Mafia**. The game alternates between Night and Day phases. Citizens must identify and eliminate all Mafia members, while Mafia must eliminate Citizens until they equal or outnumber them.

## Game Setup

1. **Minimum Players**: 3 players required to start
2. **Room System**: 
   - Create a new room or join with a 6-character room code
   - Share the room code with other players
   - Room creator can start the game when ready

3. **Role Distribution**:
   - Approximately 1/3 of players are Mafia
   - Special roles (Detective, Doctor) are assigned based on player count
   - Remaining players are Citizens

## Roles

### Citizen
- **Night Action**: None
- **Day Action**: Vote to eliminate suspected Mafia
- **Goal**: Help identify and eliminate all Mafia members
- **Win Condition**: All Mafia eliminated

### Mafia
- **Night Action**: Vote with other Mafia members to eliminate a player
- **Day Action**: Vote to eliminate players (can vote to protect themselves)
- **Special Ability**: Private Mafia chat during Night phase
- **Goal**: Eliminate Citizens until Mafia equal or outnumber them
- **Win Condition**: Mafia count â‰¥ Citizen count

### Detective
- **Night Action**: Investigate one player to learn if they are Mafia or not
- **Day Action**: Vote to eliminate suspected Mafia
- **Goal**: Use investigation results to help Citizens identify Mafia
- **Win Condition**: All Mafia eliminated (Citizens win)

### Doctor
- **Night Action**: Protect one player from death (including yourself)
- **Day Action**: Vote to eliminate suspected Mafia
- **Goal**: Save players from Mafia attacks
- **Win Condition**: All Mafia eliminated (Citizens win)

## Game Phases

### Night Phase (60 seconds)
- All players close their "eyes" (no public chat)
- **Mafia**: 
  - Discuss in private Mafia chat
  - Vote to eliminate one player
  - Majority vote determines the target
- **Detective**: 
  - Choose one player to investigate
  - Receive result: "is Mafia" or "is not Mafia"
- **Doctor**: 
  - Choose one player to protect
  - Protected player cannot be killed this night
- **Citizen**: 
  - No action, wait for day phase

### Day Phase (120 seconds)
- All players "wake up"
- **Moderator Bot** announces:
  - Who was killed (if anyone)
  - Who was protected (if applicable)
  - "No one was killed" (if Doctor protected the target)
- **Discussion**: 
  - Public chat is available
  - Players discuss and share information
  - Detective can share investigation results (but be careful!)
- **Voting**: 
  - All alive players vote to eliminate one player
  - Majority vote required (more than half of alive players)
  - Player with most votes is eliminated
  - If no majority, no one is eliminated

## Game Flow

1. **Lobby**: Players join and wait for game to start
2. **Role Assignment**: Roles are randomly assigned when game starts
3. **Night Phase**: Players perform night actions
4. **Day Phase**: Discussion and voting
5. **Repeat**: Continue alternating Night/Day until win condition is met
6. **Game End**: Winner is announced

## Win Conditions

### Citizens Win
- All Mafia members have been eliminated
- At least one Citizen remains alive

### Mafia Win
- Number of Mafia equals or exceeds the number of Citizens
- Game ends immediately when this condition is met

## Strategy Tips

### For Citizens
- Pay attention to voting patterns
- Share information carefully (Detective should be cautious)
- Look for inconsistencies in player behavior
- Trust the Doctor's protection choices

### For Mafia
- Blend in with Citizens during day phase
- Coordinate kills in Mafia chat during night
- Try to eliminate Detective and Doctor early
- Create suspicion about other players

### For Detective
- Investigate suspicious players
- Share results strategically (not immediately)
- Protect your identity from Mafia
- Build trust with other Citizens

### For Doctor
- Protect important players (Detective, yourself)
- Don't reveal who you protected
- Use protection strategically
- Vote carefully during day phase

## Game Rules

1. **No Cheating**: 
   - Don't share your role outside the game
   - Don't use external communication during the game
   - Follow the phase rules (no actions during wrong phase)

2. **Voting Rules**:
   - Each player can vote once per day phase
   - You can change your vote before phase ends
   - Majority vote is required for elimination

3. **Night Actions**:
   - Each role can perform one action per night
   - Actions are submitted secretly
   - Results are revealed at appropriate times

4. **Death**:
   - Dead players cannot vote or perform actions
   - Dead players cannot chat
   - Dead players' roles are revealed at game end

5. **Time Limits**:
   - Night phase: 60 seconds
   - Day phase: 120 seconds
   - Actions must be submitted before time expires

## Technical Notes

- **Connection**: Requires stable internet connection
- **Browser**: Works best on modern browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- **Mobile**: Fully responsive, can be played on phones
- **Reconnection**: If disconnected, you may rejoin if game hasn't started

## Tips for Best Experience

1. **Communication**: Use chat effectively to share information
2. **Observation**: Watch player behavior and voting patterns
3. **Deduction**: Use logic and information to identify Mafia
4. **Bluffing**: Mafia should act like Citizens
5. **Teamwork**: Citizens must work together to win

## Troubleshooting

- **Can't join room**: Check room code spelling (case-insensitive)
- **Game not starting**: Need at least 3 players
- **Actions not working**: Make sure you're in the correct phase
- **Chat not working**: Public chat only works during Day phase
- **Mafia chat not showing**: Only visible to Mafia during Night phase
